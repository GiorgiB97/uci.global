Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Who owns UCI?

    UCI has opened the share registry to accept anyone interested in being part of the company ownership. UCI is owned by many shareholders.

  • If I become a shareholder, how and where do I see or get hold of my shares?

    UCI has a shareholder back office platform operated by a third-party service provider.

    UCI has been verified by the back-office provider as a valid corporation with authorized shares.

    Shareholders are registered with the back office and can select how to receive their share certificates.

  • What sort of shares are issued?

    UCI has common shares also known as Class B / ordinary shares.

  • Is my shareholding recorded on any public registry?

    No. There is no public shareholder registry in Nevis. UCI or its appointed share management agents are responsible for updating and maintaining the shareholder registry which is strictly private.

  • Are my shares transferable; can I gift or bequeath my shares?

    Yes. You can gift or bequeath your shares.

  • Do my shares entitle me to dividends?

    Yes. Shareholders are entitled to dividends on each share they own.

  • Do I have voting rights?

    Yes. UCI shareholders have voting rights. Although the number of shares issued to shareholders will give enough voting power to vote against the wishes of the majority shareholders.

  • Can I sell my shares at any time?

    Yes. Subject to any time frame restriction applied. You can sell your shares at any time after the restriction time frame has expired.

  • What is the restriction time frame applied to my share purchases / acquisitions?

    UCI has a 6 months lock in period that restricts the sale or transfer of your shares during the initial 6 months of each shares acquisition you have or make.

  • When are dividends paid?

    UCI is committed to paying shareholder dividends on a regular basis. UCI is not restricted when it can pay dividends. Dividends are calculated and paid based on the revenues collected for each project listed in the UCI portfolio.

    While some projects are themselves restricted by law to pay out dividends to UCI once a year, other projects are not restricted.

    As and when revenues are collected by UCI, dividends will be calculated and paid to UCI shareholders.

  • How will I be paid dividends?

    Shareholders will have an option to receive dividend payments paid directly to their bank account or a bitcoin wallet address.

  • Are there any withholding taxes on dividends?

    No there are no withholding taxes paid on dividends.

  • Does UCI have to report dividends paid to any authority?

    UCI is not and will not report dividend information to any authority. Each shareholder is liable and responsible for their own tax reporting in their respective countries.

  • Will I have access to reports regarding financial information about the projects?

    Yes. UCI will make available financial reporting regarding each project P & L via the UCI shareholders back office.

  • Will I have access to audit reports regarding UCI?

    As an offshore corporation, not liable to pay taxes, UCI is not required to have an audit.

    But in the interests of full transparency and for the benefit of UCI shareholders, an annual audit will be performed by an independent, authorized auditor. The annual audit report will be made available to shareholders via the UCI shareholders back office.
