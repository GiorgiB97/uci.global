Associates & Partners Program

ASSOCIATE
PROGRAM

UCI offers a generous reward program for individuals and companies partnering with us.


ASSOCIATE

An Associate helps to promote ICOs and can refer others to purchase UCI shares.


QUALIFICATION

Anyone can become an associate. There is no qualification required.


The associate receives 20% commission, paid in tokens, on all participants they refer to purchase any ICO token offered by UCI.

The associate receives 20% commission, paid in UCI shares, on all new shareholders referred by the associate.

SENIOR
ASSOCIATE
PROGRAM

SENIOR ASSOCIATE

A Senior Associate helps to promote ICOs and can make their own share purchase deals.


QUALIFICATION

A senior associate needs to have purchased $1000 worth of UCI shares and 1 ETHER worth of ICO tokens.


The senior associate receives 20% commission on all participants they refer to purchase any ICO token offered by UCI. The associate can choose to be paid in tokens, cash & UCI shares or any combination of those.

The senior associate can structure their own deals for new UCI shareholders. The senior associate does their share deals, collect the payments, then purchases the UCI shares directly from UCI at 50% discount.

The senior associate can develop and manage their own teams of associates.

ASSOCIATE
PARTNER
PROGRAM

ASSOCIATE PARTNER

The associate partner program is for individuals and companies that can introduce a significant volume of sales for UCI shares and / or ICO tokens.


The associate partner can develop and manage their own sales teams.

The associate partner can also refer potential, associate partners and partners to UCI and receive ongoing commissions from those referrals.

The rewards at this level include owning a percentage of UCI and other bonuses.

PARTNER
PROGRAM

PARTNER PROGRAM

The partner program is for individuals and companies that can introduce high volumes of sales within an agreed time frame.


The partner can develop and manage their own sales teams.

The partner can also refer potential associate partners and partners to UCI and receive ongoing commissions from those referrals.

The rewards at this level include owning a higher percentage of UCI and advisory role with increasing bonuses.
