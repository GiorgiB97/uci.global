Registered UCI shareholders are rewarded 25% commission on all shares purchased by people they introduce. Full details available in UCI shareholders backoffice.

When you are ready to buy shares, please send an email with subject “BUY SHARES” kindly include the amount of shares you want to buy, and the required ID information listed on this page to:

SHAREHOLDERS@UCI.GLOBAL

How to register a UCI ShareHolder account
  • We require the following information to be included in your email.
  • First & last name or business name if a company.
  • Current physical address.
  • By law we require a colored photocopy of a government ID that has a photo of you on it.
  • Accepted ID can be any of the following:
  • Government issued ID card.
  • Drivers license.
  • Passport.
  • Please attach one of the above to your email..
  • Please allow up to 10 business days for your account to be created and the shares added.
  • Please see the information about shareholders platform.
  • Please see the information about how to claim 1000 free UCI COIN on the portfolio/CoinCrowd page.
